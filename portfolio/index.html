Introduction

I identify as biracial and I'm hoping to double major in computer science and African and African American studies, before spending some time in the field and returning to get a master's degree in computer science. You never know if you don't try!

Julie Crosse, my mother
My training in university has primarily dealt with backend engineering but I've long since had an interest in web design. Because of this I wanted to take this passion into my own hands and begin learning on the side of my actual studies. You may be wondering why I wouldn't just take a web design class at my university. The answer is this: I frankly wanted to experiment with how I experienced learning in a self paced and self regulated environment. While I don't have the assistance of a TA or professor on this journey, I also can suit my learning to my needs.
